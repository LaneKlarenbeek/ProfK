Some of my interests include:

  • Music:

    • I have enjoyed music for as long as I can remember, specifically singing. Throughout all my years of school I have been a member of a choir, as well as participiting in several honor choirs and South Dakota All-State Choir. Music is a very important part of my life and every day I am learning new things and furthering my knowledge of the subject.

  • Reading

    • I have always really enjoyed reading. Reading kept me busy as a kid because as you'd expect, living in a small town does not have a lot of entertainment to offer. A lot of things that I enjoy today stems from things that I read growing up. I used to read a lot of Science Fiction, Fiction and Fantasy. However, lately I have been really interested in biographies and Non-Fiction/Historical Non-Fiction. I believe that reading is a very important activity that everyone should do.

  • Running

    • I began running in early 2020 in the midst of the pandemic when my high school closed. Initially I used it as a way to stay healthy while being stuck inside, however, I grew to love running and did it for run rather than necessity. I still love running and do it to this day.
