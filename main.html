Hello, My name is Lane Klarenbeek and welcome to my webpage!

I grew up in a small town in South Dakota called Hudson. Being raised in a small town taught me a great amount about self-sustainability and hard work. I attended school in the Beresford School District, another small, tight-knit community similar to my own, and graduated in May of 2023. After graduating I moved on to Dakota State University to obtain a bachelor's degree in both Computer Science and Cyber Security. I am currently pursueing those degrees, and having so much fun in the process.
